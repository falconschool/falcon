ABOUT US

At Falcon Primary School we emphasises the need to work as a team in a caring, safe and friendly environment. Falcon Primary school has been driven by a community group with a clear vision which will nurture creative and outstanding individuals to realise their full potential, academically, socially, physically and spiritually.
Falcon Primary School was opened 7 years ago, located in Sparkbrook, Birmingham. We teach 600 students in total and have a 100% SATS pass rate.
